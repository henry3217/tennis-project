Training To Advance Your Game

At Tennis Haven our mission is to analyse your current style of play and help you reach that next level where you're able to close out matches more consitently. Our coaching staff will assess your strengths, and also find those key areas which are holding you back from reaching your maximum potential. When you've been playing tennis for a while sometimes it can be hard to find those little tweaks in your game which can have a huge impact in your performance. Our Coaches pick up on those little subtle things that you may overlook on a daily basis. Having another pair on eyes while your playing helps our Coaches assess in realtime what tweak and adjustements you need to help refine your game. Once our Coach has picked up on areas they believe need improving they will develop a individual plan suuited to your game.
