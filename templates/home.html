Strategy

A key part of tennis overlooked by many newcomers. Developing a strategy by observing your opponent in matchplay is a skill that cannot be overlooked. Many times during a game one will have to adjust their strategy.

Mental Toughness

Another very important aspect of the game is mental strength. Many Pro tennis players have compared tennis to boxing, it can be a lonely sport at times. Therefore learning to be mentally tough plays a great importance on ones chances for victory.

Fitness Plan

Fitness plays a very important role in a tennis players game. The fitter the individual the higher the success rate of that individual grinding out those long games. As a tennis player time needs to be spent in the gym in order to strengthen the core muscles in the body.

Coaching

Are you a beginner or intermediate player looking to take your game to the next level? Do you struggle to close out matches when you're leading and in control? A lot of players at club level face these challenges, at Tennis Haven our role is to help you step up in these moments and help close out those tight matches that you're struggling with. We use a number of different coaching techniques to help our students feel more confident on the court.
